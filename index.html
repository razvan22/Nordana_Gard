Nordanå Gård

You live here in one of our 2 small houses as well as 2 larger houses that were built in 2016. There is everything you need in a compact style. Here is the calm and proximity to nature. But at the same time also the proximity to Denmark and the big city by train. The sea and Åhus are 35 min away by car, but the pool here is good 20 m from the house.

There is also a kitchen with laundry room and dishwasher. We also have a party room, My wife and I have been living here on the farm since 2007 after my parents have divided the farm since 1964. The land remains but most of it is rented out.I myself work with network installations like regular work and farm at other times. Training has been a great leisure activity when I did not have this project. At 800m distance from the farm there are nice footpaths in the forest.

In Kristianstad we have Naturum in the water kingdom and the railway museum, also the cradle of the film is in Kristianstad and as a museum. Outside Knislinge we have Vanås castle with various art exhibitions. Ignaberga has limestone caves. We also have close proximity to Vinslöv and the cult film "Suddenly in Vinslöv" and Flädergården also in Vinslöv
